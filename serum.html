    • Serum Dos and Don’ts

    Do: Wash hair before application

    Hair serum is typically a leave-in product and best applied to freshly shampooed hair. The serum’s primary purpose is to protect the hair from pollutants. Using it on unwashed hair defeats the purpose. Moreover, applying it on unwashed hair can make the hair look oily and weighed down. After a person washes their hair, they can towel dry it and then apply the product. A person can also use some after taking a shower at night for a more glossy, soft hair in the morning. However, a person may also use hair serums to touch up their hair throughout the day.

    Do: Warm it up before applying to the hair

    Most hair serums have a thick consistency, making them hard to apply. Warming up the product by placing a little product on the palm and rubbing it for a few seconds can make it smoother, which helps ensure a more even application.
